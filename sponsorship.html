Sponsorship

Interested in becoming a sponsor?

Swift Weekly Brief is distributed every Thursday to thousands of savvy Swift developers and enthusiasts. Each issue contains quality links and commentary on what's happening in the Swift.org open source projects, as well as what's happening in the Swift community at large.

Sponsored Links

There is a single Sponsored Link available per issue for $75. This is featured prominently at the beginning of an issue, after the opening comments and before the first headline. These links are great to get products or services noticed by subscribers. Sponsored Links should be relevant to the Swift developer community. Additionally, a tweet will be sent out from the Swift Weekly Brief twitter account thanking the sponsor.
